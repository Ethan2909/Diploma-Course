My Story.

My name is Ethan Taylor and I am a web designer and developer.

I have been self learning for the last 18months to 2 years, specialising in front end design. My passion for web design came about from my love of building computers. When I bought my first SSD to have my boot drive on, I bricked it. I ended up staying up till like 4 in the morning learning cmd prompt and how I could back door it and reset the drive back to factory settings, thankfully I was successful and I still use that drive today. From that love of computers, sprung my love to create things, whether that be a lego tower for my child or a fully functional website.

Other than learning code, building websites, my other loves in live are my family, friends, gaming, motor sports and BMX racing.
